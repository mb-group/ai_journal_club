## Trajectory Analysis During Perturbation

### Developmental Trajectory Modeling

#### CellOT: Optimal Transport for Cell Trajectories
- **Description**: Optimal transport methods for analyzing cell trajectory during perturbations
- **Input**: Time-course single-cell data with perturbations
- **Output**: Optimal transport maps and trajectory predictions
- **Paper**: Nature Methods 2023
- **Application**: Developmental biology, drug response, cell reprogramming

#### TrajectoryNet: Neural Networks for Trajectory Analysis
- **Description**: Neural network approaches for modeling developmental trajectories
- **Input**: Single-cell trajectory data
- **Output**: Trajectory predictions and branch point analysis
- **Paper**: ICML 2020
- **Application**: Development, differentiation, cell fate decisions

### Developmental Timing Analysis

#### Developmental Time and Tempo Analysis
- **Description**: Computational methods for analyzing developmental timing and tempo
- **Input**: Time-series developmental data
- **Output**: Developmental timing predictions and tempo analysis
- **Paper**: Nature Methods 2023
- **Application**: Development biology, aging, disease timing
