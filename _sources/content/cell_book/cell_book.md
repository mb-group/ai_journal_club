# Cell Book

![Cell Book Cover](../../assets/cell-bookcover.jpg)

A comprehensive collection of artificial intelligence and machine learning approaches applied to cell biology, from molecular mechanisms to cellular behavior in tissues. This book covers computational methods for understanding cells at multiple scales and contexts.

## Part 1: Introduction to Cell Biology

1. **[Cells and Genomes](chapter1_cells_genomes.md)** - Cell type-specific embeddings and foundation models
2. **[Cell Chemistry and Biosynthesis](chapter2_cell_chemistry.md)** - Cellular biochemical processes
3. **[Proteins](chapter3_proteins.md)** - Protein function and essentiality in cellular context

## Part 2: Basic Genetic Mechanisms

4. **[DNA and Chromosomes](chapter4_dna_chromosomes.md)** - Chromatin organization and structure
5. **[DNA Replication, Repair, Recombination](chapter5_dna_replication.md)** - DNA maintenance mechanisms
6. **[How Cells Read the Genome: From DNA to Protein](chapter6_genome_to_protein.md)** - Gene expression and regulation
7. **[Control of Gene Expression](chapter7_gene_expression_control.md)** - Regulatory networks and cell-type specificity

## Part 3: Methods

8. **[Manipulating Proteins, DNA, and RNA](chapter8_manipulation_methods.md)** - Designer tools and spatial analysis
9. **[Visualizing Cells](chapter9_visualizing_cells.md)** - Computational visualization and trajectory analysis
