```{admonition} Evaluation Criteria
:class: note

For each tool in our catalog, we consider:
- **Ease of use**: Installation and learning curve
- **Documentation**: Quality and completeness
- **Validation**: Published benchmarks and case studies
- **Community**: Active development and support
- **Computational requirements**: Hardware needs and scalability
```
