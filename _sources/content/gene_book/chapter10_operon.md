This chapter covers computational approaches to analyzing bacterial operons and prokaryotic gene regulation.

## Operon Prediction

### Bacterial Gene Clustering
- **Description**: Computational prediction of operons in bacterial genomes
- **Input**: Bacterial genomic sequences
- **Output**: Operon boundaries and gene organization
- **Application**: Bacterial genomics, systems biology

## Prokaryotic Regulation

### Transcriptional Control Elements
- **Description**: Prediction of prokaryotic promoters and regulatory elements
- **Input**: Bacterial DNA sequences
- **Output**: Regulatory element predictions
- **Application**: Bacterial gene regulation analysis
