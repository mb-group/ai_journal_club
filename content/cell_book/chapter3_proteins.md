## Protein Embedding with Cell Context

### Cell-Context Aware Protein Models

#### PINNACLE: Protein Embedding with Cell Context
- **Description**: Protein embedding model that incorporates cellular context
- **Input**: Protein sequences with cellular environment information
- **Output**: Cell-context aware protein embeddings
- **GitHub**: Available
- **Paper**: Nature Methods 2024
- **Application**: Context-specific protein function prediction, cell-type-specific protein analysis

#### ProtGO: Function-Guided Protein Representation
- **Description**: Function-guided approach to protein representation learning
- **Input**: Protein sequences and functional annotations
- **Output**: Function-informed protein representations
- **Paper**: NIPS 2024
- **Application**: Protein function prediction, functional analysis

## Protein Essentiality Analysis

### Essential Protein Prediction

#### PIC: Protein Importance Calculator
- **Description**: Predicts protein essentiality at human cell-line level
- **Input**: Protein sequences
- **Output**: Essentiality scores for proteins in specific cell lines
- **GitHub**: Available
- **Paper**: Nature Computational Science 2024
- **Core Technology**: Protein Pre-trained Language Models (PLM)
- **Model Usage**: 
  - Input: Protein sequence
  - Output: Essentiality score
- **Training**:
  - Input: Protein sequences with essentiality labels
  - Output: Trained essentiality prediction model
- **Application**: Drug target identification, genetic screening, therapeutic development
