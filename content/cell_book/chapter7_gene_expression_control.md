# Chapter 7: Control of Gene Expression

## Regulatory Element Prediction

### Transcriptional Regulatory Elements

#### Sei: Sequence-to-Expression Prediction
- **Description**: Deep learning model for predicting gene expression from sequence
- **Input**: DNA sequences
- **Output**: Expression predictions and regulatory element identification
- **Paper**: Available
- **Application**: Regulatory genomics, variant effect prediction

#### GET: Gene Expression Transformer
- **Description**: Transformer-based model for gene expression prediction
- **Input**: Genomic sequences and regulatory information
- **Output**: Gene expression predictions
- **Paper**: Available
- **Application**: Expression modeling, regulatory analysis

## Cell Type-Specific Gene Regulatory Networks

### scGraphETM: Single-Cell Graph Expression Topic Model
- **Description**: Graph-based topic modeling for single-cell gene expression
- **Input**: Single-cell expression data
- **Output**: Cell-type-specific regulatory networks and gene modules
- **Paper**: Available
- **Application**: Cell-type-specific regulation analysis, developmental biology

### Cell-Type Agnostic Regulatory Prediction

#### Universal Regulatory Models
- **Description**: Models for predicting gene regulation across cell types
- **Input**: Multi-cell-type expression and regulatory data
- **Output**: Cell-type agnostic regulatory predictions
- **Paper**: Cell Genomics 2025
- **Application**: Universal regulatory principles, cross-cell-type analysis
