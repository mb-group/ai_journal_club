## Introduction

This section contains detailed case studies examining real-world applications of AI in biological research. Each case study provides:

- **Context**: The biological problem and why AI is being applied
- **What Works**: Documented successes and their limitations
- **What Doesn't**: Common failure modes and misconceptions
- **Practical Implications**: What these findings mean for your research
