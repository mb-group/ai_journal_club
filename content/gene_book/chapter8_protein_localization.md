# Chapter 8: Protein Localization

This chapter covers computational prediction of protein subcellular localization and trafficking.

## Subcellular Localization Prediction

### Signal Peptide Prediction
- **Description**: Computational prediction of signal peptides and targeting sequences
- **Input**: Protein sequences
- **Output**: Localization predictions and signal sequence identification
- **Application**: Cell biology, protein engineering

## Membrane Protein Analysis

### Transmembrane Domain Prediction
- **Description**: Prediction of transmembrane domains and membrane topology
- **Input**: Protein sequences
- **Output**: Membrane topology predictions
- **Application**: Membrane protein structure and function analysis
