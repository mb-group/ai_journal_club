# Chapter 5: Messenger RNA

This chapter covers computational approaches to mRNA analysis, processing, and regulation.

## mRNA Structure and Processing

### mRNA Secondary Structure Prediction
- **Description**: Computational prediction of mRNA secondary structures
- **Input**: mRNA sequences
- **Output**: Secondary structure predictions
- **Application**: mRNA stability analysis, regulatory element identification

## mRNA Localization and Stability

### mRNA-LM Applications
- **Description**: Applications of mRNA language models for stability and localization
- **Input**: mRNA sequences
- **Output**: Stability and localization predictions
- **Paper**: NAR 2025
- **Application**: mRNA therapeutics design

## UTR Analysis

### 5' and 3' UTR Regulation
- **Description**: Computational analysis of untranslated region regulation
- **Input**: UTR sequences
