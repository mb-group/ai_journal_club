## Protein-DNA Interactions

### Binding Specificity Prediction

#### ESM-DBP: ESM for DNA-Binding Proteins
- **Description**: ESM-based model for DNA-binding protein analysis
- **Input**: Protein sequences
- **Output**: DNA-binding predictions
- **Application**: Protein-DNA interaction prediction

#### DeepPBS: Deep Protein-Binding Specificity
- **Description**: Deep learning model for predicting protein-DNA binding specificity
- **Input**: Protein-DNA binding structures
- **Output**: Binding specificity predictions
- **GitHub**: Available
- **Paper**: Nature Methods 2024
- **Application**: Transcription factor binding site prediction

### Protein-Nucleic Acid Complexes

#### Protein-Nucleic Acid Complex Prediction
- **Description**: Models for predicting protein-nucleic acid complex structures
- **Input**: Protein and nucleic acid sequences
- **Output**: Complex structure predictions
- **Paper**: NIPS 2024
- **Application**: Structural biology, drug design

## Epigenetics

### DNA Methylation Models

#### MethylGPT: GPT for DNA Methylation
- **Description**: GPT-based model for DNA methylation analysis
- **Input**: DNA sequences and methylation patterns
- **Output**: Methylation predictions
- **Paper**: Available
- **Application**: Epigenetic analysis, disease prediction

#### GcGPT: GC Content and Methylation
- **Description**: GPT model focusing on GC content and methylation relationships
- **Input**: DNA sequences with GC content information
- **Output**: Methylation and expression predictions
- **Paper**: Available
- **Application**: Epigenetic regulation analysis

## RNA Processing and Regulation

### Internal Ribosome Entry Sites

#### IRES Prediction in Cellular and Viral mRNA
- **Description**: Computational prediction of internal ribosome entry sites
- **Input**: mRNA sequences
- **Output**: IRES site predictions
- **Paper**: Bioinformatics 2024
- **Application**: Translation regulation, viral biology

### Alternative Splicing

#### SpTransformer: Splice Site Prediction
- **Description**: Transformer model for tissue-specific splicing prediction
- **Input**: Pre-mRNA sequences
- **Output**: Tissue-specific splicing site predictions
- **GitHub**: Available
- **Paper**: Nature Communication 2024
- **Application**: Alternative splicing analysis, disease mechanisms
