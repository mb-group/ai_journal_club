# Chapter 25: Pathogens

## Host-Pathogen Interactions

### Viral Pathogen Analysis

#### E2VD: Early Detection of Viral Diseases
- **Description**: Early detection system for viral diseases using computational approaches
- **Input**: Clinical and molecular data from patients
- **Output**: Early viral disease detection and classification
- **Paper**: Nature Machine Intelligence 2025
- **Application**: Pandemic preparedness, early diagnosis, public health surveillance

## Pathogen Genomics

### Viral Evolution Analysis

#### Viral Mutation Tracking
- **Description**: Computational tracking of viral mutations and evolution
- **Input**: Viral genomic sequences over time
- **Output**: Mutation patterns and evolutionary predictions
- **Application**: Vaccine design, drug resistance monitoring, epidemiology

### Bacterial Pathogen Analysis

#### Antibiotic Resistance Prediction
- **Description**: Computational prediction of antibiotic resistance in bacterial pathogens
- **Input**: Bacterial genomic data and resistance profiles
- **Output**: Resistance mechanism predictions
- **Application**: Antibiotic stewardship, treatment optimization
