## Cell Trajectory Analysis

### Developmental Trajectory Inference

#### CellOracle: Cell Fate Prediction
- **Description**: Computational framework for predicting cell fate transitions
- **Input**: Single-cell RNA-seq data and regulatory networks
- **Output**: Cell fate predictions and trajectory analysis
- **Paper**: Nature 2023
- **Application**: Development biology, cell reprogramming, regenerative medicine

#### CellRank2: Advanced Trajectory Analysis
- **Description**: Advanced methods for cell trajectory and fate analysis
- **Input**: Single-cell data with temporal information
- **Output**: Cell state transitions and fate probabilities
- **Paper**: Nature Methods 2024
- **Application**: Developmental biology, disease progression analysis

## Cell Communication Visualization

### Intercellular Communication Analysis

#### CytoCommunity: Cell Communication Networks
- **Description**: Framework for analyzing and visualizing cell-cell communication
- **Input**: Single-cell data with spatial information
- **Output**: Cell communication networks and signaling pathways
- **Paper**: Nature Methods 2024
- **Application**: Tissue biology, cell communication analysis, disease mechanisms
