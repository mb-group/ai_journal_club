# Protein Book

![Proteins Book Cover](../../assets/proteins-creighton-book-cover.jpeg)

Accumulated 69 papers since 2024

This book covers various aspects of protein science through the lens of artificial intelligence and machine learning approaches. Each chapter focuses on different aspects of protein structure, function, and behavior.

## Chapters Overview

1. **[Chemical Properties of Polypeptides](chapter1_chemical_properties.md)** - Thermal stability and mutation effects
2. **[Biosynthesis of Proteins](chapter2_biosynthesis.md)** - Protein synthesis mechanisms
3. **[Evolutionary and Genetic Origins](chapter3_evolutionary_origins.md)** - Protein language models and evolution
4. **[Physical Interactions](chapter4_physical_interactions.md)** - Protein-protein and protein-peptide interactions
5. **[Conformational Properties](chapter5_conformational_properties.md)** - Peptide conformations and IDR analysis
6. **[Folded Conformations](chapter6_folded_conformations.md)** - Globular protein structures
7. **[Proteins in Solution and Membranes](chapter7_proteins_in_solution.md)** - GPCR and membrane proteins
8. **[Interactions with Other Molecules](chapter8_interactions_with_molecules.md)** - Molecular interactions
9. **[Enzyme Catalysis](chapter9_enzyme_catalysis.md)** - Catalytic mechanisms and binding sites
10. **[Degradation](chapter10_degradation.md)** - Protein degradation processes
