This chapter covers computational approaches to RNA splicing, alternative splicing, and post-transcriptional processing.

## Alternative Splicing Analysis

### SpTransformer: Tissue-Specific Splicing
- **Description**: Transformer model for predicting tissue-specific splicing patterns
- **Input**: Pre-mRNA sequences and tissue context
- **Output**: Tissue-specific splicing site predictions
- **GitHub**: Available
- **Paper**: Nature Communication 2024
- **Application**: Understanding tissue-specific gene regulation and disease mechanisms

## Splicing Regulatory Networks

### Splicing Factor Binding Prediction
- **Description**: Computational prediction of splicing factor binding sites
- **Input**: RNA sequences and protein binding data
- **Output**: Binding site predictions and regulatory networks
- **Application**: Splicing regulation analysis, therapeutic target identification

## Post-Transcriptional Modifications

### RNA Modification Prediction
- **Description**: Computational prediction of RNA modifications and their effects
- **Input**: RNA sequences
- **Output**: Modification site predictions and functional effects
- **Application**: Epitranscriptomics, RNA therapeutics
