# Chapter 12: Phage Strategies

This chapter covers computational approaches to analyzing bacteriophage biology and viral gene regulation strategies.

## Viral Gene Regulation

### Phage Lifecycle Prediction
- **Description**: Computational modeling of phage lifecycle decisions
- **Input**: Phage genomic sequences and host conditions
- **Output**: Lifecycle pathway predictions
- **Application**: Phage biology, biotechnology applications

## Host-Pathogen Interactions

### CRISPR-Cas System Analysis
- **Description**: Computational analysis of CRISPR-Cas systems and phage resistance
- **Input**: Bacterial and phage genomes
- **Output**: CRISPR target predictions and resistance mechanisms
- **Application**: Phage therapy, genome editing
