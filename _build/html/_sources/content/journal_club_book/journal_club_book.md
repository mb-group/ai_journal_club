## Purpose

Our journal club discussions often reveal patterns, connections, and insights that go beyond individual papers. This book serves as:

- **Conceptual Clarity**: Clear explanations of frequently confusing AI concepts
- **Historical Context**: Documentation of the most important milestone works that shaped the field
- **Foundational Knowledge**: Essential AI concepts every biologist should understand
- **Trend Analysis**: Important shifts in direction, methodology, and focus areas

Each section represents the distilled wisdom of multiple discussions, debates, and collaborative analysis sessions.
