# Selection Criteria

## Topics Prioritized

Topics are mostly around Babu group research interests, such as IDR, GPCR, peptide, subcellular localization towards the long-term goal of understanding how cell functions. Works around Genes and The Cell are selected.

## Journals and Conference as Source

- Prioritized for Nature and Science Portfolio publications in 2025, with a few from earlier years till 2022
- A few Top AI conference (NIPS, ICLR, ICML, AAAI, etc.) 2025 works are selected from their spotlight lists
- Other journals are only sporadically included
- New works coming out in 2025 will be gradually selected and implemented to the playground
